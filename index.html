Welcome to the Smart Formations Roadmap!

As an incubation project of ThirdStrandStudio , we leverage their expertise in technology and business development to stay ahead of industry trends and deliver exceptional value to our clients. Smart Formations is committed to providing seamless and efficient company formation services globally. Here is an overview of our development progress and future milestones. This roadmap highlights our journey from inception to the continuous enhancements we are implementing to serve you better..

Phase 1: Concept and Initial Development

Smart Formations was conceived as an incubation project of ThirdStrandStudio, aiming to revolutionize company formation and registration services using cutting-edge technology.

Market Research: Conducted extensive market research to identify pain points in existing services and opportunities for innovation, particularly in the blockchain and web3 sectors.

Prototype Development: Developed an initial prototype of our AI-driven company formation platform, focusing on user experience and automation capabilities.

Phase 2: Beta Launch and Feedback

Beta Version Release: Launched a beta version of Smart Formations to a select group of early adopters, allowing us to gather valuable feedback and make iterative improvements.

User Testing and Feedback Integration: Conducted thorough testing and integrated user feedback to enhance functionality, streamline the registration process, and ensure reliability.

Expansion of Services: Added initial services including company registration, legal document templates, and consulting for specific industries.

Phase 3: Official Launch and Expansion

Official Launch: Successfully launched Smart Formations to the public, providing comprehensive company formation services in key jurisdictions such as the UK, USA (Delaware and Wyoming), Marshall Islands, Switzerland, BVI, and Cayman Islands.

AI-Powered Customization: Rolled out our AI-powered tool that assesses customer business requirements and designs custom SPVs tailored to their needs.

Blockchain Integration: Implemented blockchain-based registration options, expediting the company formation process and enhancing security.

Phase 4: Service Enhancements and Global Expansion

Enhanced Service Offerings: Expanded our service portfolio to include bank account opening and advanced web3 DAO incorporation services.

Global Reach: Planned expansion to additional jurisdictions, broadening our global footprint to better serve international businesses and entrepreneurs.

Customer Support Improvements: Introduced 24/7 customer support and a comprehensive help center to assist clients at every step of their journey.

Phase 5: Future Innovations

Advanced AI Capabilities: Continuously enhancing our AI tools to provide more accurate and tailored business solutions.

New Market Penetration: Exploring entry into emerging markets and offering specialized services to cater to new business landscapes.

Community and Ecosystem Development: Building a robust community around Smart Formations, fostering collaboration, and integrating new technologies to support decentralized business models.
